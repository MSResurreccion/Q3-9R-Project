Rob Deniel


Rob was then a 17-year-old newcomer basking in the success of his debut recording, Ulap. Six million hits on YouTube upon release late in 2020. That was when he claimed that his music genre was bedroom jazz with shades of rock. Promising newcomer.

Rob Deniel Barrinuevo (born October 6, 2003) know by his stage name Rob Deniel is a Filipino singer-songwriter under Viva Records. He debuted on June 8, 2020 with his first single, "Baby I Tried".

On June 8, 2020 he made his solo debut with his first digital single “Ulap”.

In October 2020 after his song “Ulap” which was originally released on June 8, 2020 went viral on his soundcloud and youtube account, Rob signed with Viva company as a new artist in said company and label.

The young hotshot singer-songwriter and multi-instrumentalist Rob Deniel made an auto-hit with his most popular track, "Ulap" which was all written, arranged, and produced by Rob himself. Two other songs of his that he put out are the indie-pop flavored “Gabi” which says a lot about Rob’s leanings and style.

An elaboration of his omnific mind is the singles titled "Diwa” and "Darling, Darling" that maintained his momentum on the most-streamed artists at present. Of course, he continues to bring some old-timey styled soul on his tracks titled “Kundiman” and "Sinta" that mixes retro pop sounds and soul-balladry with yearning sentiments that definitely helped his growth as an artist.

Of course, Rob doesn't stop there as he continues to rock our world with his recently released single titled "Ang Pag-Ibig" that immediately went bubbling under Spotify’s popular Viral 50 Philippines playlist as he rose at the 15th spot and has now more than 22 million streams on Spotify alone.
